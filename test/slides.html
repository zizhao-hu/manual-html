A five-minute story

Meridian Coffee

Small-batch roastery on Lamplight Ave.

1 / 5

The beans

We source from one farm at a time.

Every Monday, a new single-origin arrives green. We taste it, roast three sample profiles, and pick the one that best shows what that farm did right this season.

  • Direct relationships with twelve farms across four countries.
  • One origin on the bar per week, never blended.
  • Lot codes printed on every bag so you can trace the cup back.

2 / 5

The roast

Light enough to taste the farm.

We roast in a 2kg Probat with a 14–16 minute profile. Development time is short, the drop temperature is low. You get fruit and acidity, not char.

3 / 5

The brew

Four ways to drink it.

  • Batch drip — the cleanest expression, brewed at 1:17.
  • Pourover — V60, 22 grams, 360 grams of water, 3:30 total.
  • Espresso — 20 in, 45 out, 28 seconds.
  • Cold brew — 18 hours at 4°C, cut with still water.

4 / 5
